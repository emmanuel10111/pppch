Official Rules

Complete sweepstakes rules and free entry instructions.

Last updated: May 1, 2026

📬 No Purchase Necessary. A purchase will not improve your chances of winning. See free entry instructions below.

Eligibility

PCH Shopping giveaways are open only to individuals who: (i) are legal residents of the United States; (ii) are physically located and residing within the United States at the time of entry; (iii) are twenty-one (21) years of age or older at the time of entry; (iv) have a valid residential street address (no P.O. Boxes for prize delivery); and (v) satisfy all other eligibility requirements herein. Employees and immediate family members of PCH Shopping and its affiliates are not eligible.

How to Enter — Purchase Method

Visit pchshopping.com and make an eligible product purchase. Every $1 spent (excluding taxes and shipping) earns one (1) sweepstakes entry into all active giveaways. Entries are credited to your account automatically upon purchase confirmation.

How to Enter — Free Mail-In Method

To enter without a purchase, hand-print your full name, complete mailing address (street, city, state, ZIP), date of birth, and the specific Giveaway Number on a plain 3"×5" card. Mail it in a stamped envelope to:

PCH Shopping — [Giveaway Number]
Free Entry Department
382 Channel Drive
Port Washington, NY 11050

Limit: one (1) hand-printed free mail entry per outer mailing envelope. Mechanically reproduced entries are void. All entries must be postmarked by the entry deadline and received within 7 days thereafter.

Prize Descriptions

  • GWY #22915 — $1,000,000 SuperPrize: Paid as $5,000/week for 200 weeks (approximately $1,000,000), or winner may elect a lump-sum cash alternative of lesser value. Ends May 31, 2026.
  • GWY #22926 — Jeep® Wagoneer + $10,000: One (1) Jeep® Wagoneer (approximate retail value $65,000) plus $10,000 cash, or cash alternative of $75,000. Ends May 16, 2026.
  • GWY #23093 — BMW 230i Coupe: One (1) BMW 230i Coupe (approximate retail value $45,000) or cash alternative of $45,000. Ends June 4, 2026.
  • GWY #23089 — Subaru® Outback: One (1) Subaru® Outback (approximate retail value $43,965) or cash alternative of $43,965. Ends May 7, 2026.

Winner Selection

Winners will be selected by random drawing from all eligible entries received by the entry deadline. Drawings will be conducted by an independent judging organization within 30 days of each entry deadline. Odds of winning depend upon the total number of eligible entries received.

Winner Notification

Potential winners will be notified by email, phone, and/or mail. Potential winners must respond within 14 days of notification. Failure to respond within the required time may result in forfeiture of the prize and selection of an alternate winner.

Taxes

All federal, state, and local taxes on prizes are the sole responsibility of winners. Winners may be required to complete a W-9 tax form. Prizes valued at $600 or more will be reported to the IRS.

General Conditions

PCH Shopping reserves the right to cancel, suspend, or modify any giveaway if fraud, technical failures, or other factors impair the integrity of the promotion. All decisions of PCH Shopping are final. Void where prohibited by law.

Governing Law

These Official Rules are governed by the laws of the State of New York, without regard to conflict of law provisions. Any disputes shall be resolved in the courts of Nassau County, New York.
