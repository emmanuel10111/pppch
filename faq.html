Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PCH Shopping, giveaways, and winning.

General

What is PCH Shopping? ▾
PCH Shopping is an online shopping platform where every dollar you spend earns sweepstakes entries into exclusive prize giveaways — including cash, cars, and our $1,000,000 SuperPrize. Shop products you love and enter to win life-changing prizes at the same time.
Do I have to buy something to enter? ▾
No! No purchase is necessary to enter or win. You can enter any giveaway for free by mail. See the Official Rules for each giveaway for free entry instructions. A purchase does not improve your odds of winning.
Who is eligible to enter? ▾
Giveaways are open to legal residents of the United States who are 21 years of age or older at the time of entry and physically located within the United States. See the Official Rules for each giveaway for complete eligibility requirements.
Are the giveaways real? ▾
Absolutely. PCH has awarded over $492 million in prizes to real people across America. Every giveaway has official rules, real winners, and verified prize fulfillment. Check out our Winners Circle to see real recent winners.

Entries & Winning

How do I earn sweepstakes entries? ▾
Every $1 you spend at PCH Shopping earns 1 automatic entry into all active giveaways. Memberships give you up to 20,000 automatic entries per month. You can also buy Reward Packs for one-time entry boosts, and spin our daily Spin & Win wheel for bonus prizes.
How does the Spin & Win wheel work? ▾
Every day you get one free spin on our Spin & Win prize wheel. Every spin wins$200,000 or more — guaranteed! Prizes range from$200,000 all the way up to $1,000,000. Just click the wheel on the homepage to spin.
What are my odds of winning? ▾
Odds depend on the total number of eligible entries received for each giveaway. All eligible entries have an equal chance of winning. Members with more auto-entries have proportionally more entries, but no one can guarantee a win. See the Official Rules for each giveaway for complete odds disclosures.
How will I be notified if I win? ▾
Winners are notified by email, phone, and/or mail using the contact information provided at registration. For major prizes, the PCH Prize Patrol may visit you in person! Make sure your contact information is current in your account settings.
Can I choose cash instead of a vehicle prize? ▾
Yes! For vehicle and other major prize giveaways, winners are typically offered a cash alternative option. The specific cash alternative amount is listed on each giveaway's page and in the Official Rules.

Memberships

What membership plans are available? ▾
We offer three monthly membership tiers: Gold ($25/month — 2,000 auto entries), Platinum ($60/month — 6,000 auto entries), and Diamond ($150/month — 20,000 auto entries). All include entry multipliers, merch discounts, and PCH CashBack access.
Can I cancel my membership? ▾
Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you keep all benefits through the end of the period you've already paid for. No partial refunds are issued.
What is PCH CashBack? ▾
PCH CashBack is our rewards platform where you can earn cash back on eligible purchases and online activities. All registered PCH Shopping users and members have access to the CashBack platform.

Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take? ▾
Standard shipping typically takes 5–10 business days. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout. Orders are processed within 1–2 business days. See our Shipping Policy for full details.
What is your return policy? ▾
We offer a 30-day return policy on most items. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and returned within 30 days of delivery. See our Refund Policy for complete details and any category-specific exceptions.
