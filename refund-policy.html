Refund Policy

Our 30-day hassle-free return guarantee.

Last updated: May 1, 2026

🛡️ We offer a 30-day return window on most products. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Returns

You have 30 days from the date of delivery to return most items for a full refund. Items must be unused, in their original condition, and in original packaging. To initiate a return, contact our support team at contact page with your order number and reason for return.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will notify you by email. If approved, your refund will be processed to your original payment method within 5–10 business days. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Items returned more than 30 days after delivery
  • Items that are used, damaged, or missing original packaging
  • Downloadable digital products (Reward Pack digital wallpapers)
  • Membership subscription fees (pro-rated refunds not available)
  • Personalized or custom-made items

Exchanges

If you received a defective or damaged item, we will replace it at no cost to you. Contact us within 30 days of delivery with photos of the damage.

Membership Cancellations

Monthly memberships can be cancelled at any time from your account settings. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. Partial-month refunds are not available. Your sweepstakes entries earned during the membership period remain valid.

Contact Us

For return or refund questions, visit our contact page or email pchofficials391@gmail.com. Please include your order number for fastest service.
