Shipping Policy

Fast, reliable delivery on all PCH Shopping orders.

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days (Monday through Friday, excluding US federal holidays). Orders placed after 2pm EST may be processed the following business day.

Shipping Options

  • Standard Shipping (5–10 business days): FREE on orders over $50. $5.99 on orders under $50.
  • Expedited Shipping (2–3 business days): $12.99 flat rate, available at checkout.
  • Overnight Shipping (next business day): $24.99, available on select items.

Shipping Destinations

We currently ship to all 50 US states and Washington D.C. We do not ship to US territories, APO/FPO addresses, or international destinations at this time.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your package directly on the carrier's website. Most orders are shipped via USPS, UPS, or FedEx.

Lost or Damaged Shipments

If your order arrives damaged or is lost in transit, please contact us within 14 days of the expected delivery date. We will work with the carrier to resolve the issue and arrange a replacement or refund as appropriate.

Contact Us

For shipping questions, visit our contact page or email pchofficials391@gmail.com.
